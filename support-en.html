‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

+

First, set up your profile and connect with your partner. Then you can register anniversaries and share memorable photos.

How do I connect with my partner?

+

Go to "Partner Connection" from your profile screen to create and share an invitation code, or enter your partner's invitation code.

I forgot my password

+

Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen and follow the reset link sent to your registered email address.

I want to delete my account

+

You can select "Delete Account" from profile settings. Please note that all data will be lost and cannot be recovered.

The app crashes

+

Please update to the latest version and restart your device. If the problem persists, contact support with your device information.

Cannot upload photos

+

Check your network connection and verify that the app has camera and file access permissions in settings.

I want to set anniversary notifications

+

Enable notifications for each anniversary in the anniversary settings screen and allow app notifications in your device settings.

I want to disconnect from my partner

+

You can select "Disconnect" from "Partner Connection" in your profile screen. After disconnecting, access to shared anniversaries and memories will be restricted.
